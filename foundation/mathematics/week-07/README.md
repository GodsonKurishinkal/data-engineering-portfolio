# Week 7: Sequences and Series

## Learning Objectives
- Understand arithmetic and geometric sequences
- Calculate sums of series
- Work with infinite series
- Apply sequences to data patterns

## Topics Covered

### 1. Sequences
- Arithmetic sequences
- Geometric sequences
- Recursive sequences
- Finding nth terms

### 2. Series
- Arithmetic series
- Geometric series
- Infinite geometric series
- Convergence and divergence

### 3. Applications
- Financial calculations
- Time series patterns
- Algorithm analysis
- Data pattern recognition

## Practice Problems

1. Find the 20th term of the arithmetic sequence: 3, 7, 11, 15, ...
2. Find the sum of the first 10 terms of the geometric sequence: 2, 6, 18, ...
3. Determine if the infinite series converges: 1 + 1/2 + 1/4 + 1/8 + ...
