# Week 5: Polynomials and Factoring

## Learning Objectives
- Perform operations with polynomials
- Factor various types of polynomials
- Use the zero-product property
- Solve polynomial equations

## Topics Covered

### 1. Polynomial Operations
- Adding and subtracting polynomials
- Multiplying polynomials
- Special products (difference of squares, perfect square trinomials)
- Polynomial division

### 2. Factoring Techniques
- Greatest common factor (GCF)
- Factoring trinomials
- Factoring by grouping
- Difference of squares and sum/difference of cubes

### 3. Solving Polynomial Equations
- Zero-product property
- Finding roots/zeros
- Quadratic formula
- Rational root theorem

## Practice Problems

1. Factor completely: x² - 9
2. Factor: 6x² + 11x - 10
3. Solve: x² - 5x + 6 = 0
