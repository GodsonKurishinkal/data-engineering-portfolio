# Week 11: Derivatives and Differentiation

## Learning Objectives
- Understand the concept of derivatives
- Calculate derivatives using various rules
- Apply derivatives to optimization problems
- Understand rates of change in data

## Topics Covered

### 1. Derivative Fundamentals
- Definition using limits
- Geometric interpretation (slope)
- Notation (dy/dx, f'(x), etc.)
- Differentiability

### 2. Differentiation Rules
- Power rule
- Product rule
- Quotient rule
- Chain rule
- Derivatives of exponential and logarithmic functions

### 3. Applications
- Rate of change
- Optimization problems
- Marginal analysis
- Gradient descent basics

## Practice Problems

1. Find the derivative of f(x) = 3x⁴ - 2x² + 5
2. Find dy/dx for y = (x² + 1)(x³ - 2)
3. Find the critical points of f(x) = x³ - 3x² + 2
