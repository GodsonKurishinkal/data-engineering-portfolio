# Week 10: Introduction to Calculus - Limits

## Learning Objectives
- Understand the concept of limits
- Evaluate limits using various methods
- Understand continuity
- Apply limits to real-world problems

## Topics Covered

### 1. Limit Fundamentals
- Intuitive understanding of limits
- One-sided limits
- Infinite limits
- Limits at infinity

### 2. Evaluating Limits
- Direct substitution
- Factoring and simplifying
- Rationalization
- L'Hôpital's rule (introduction)

### 3. Continuity
- Definition of continuity
- Types of discontinuities
- Intermediate value theorem
- Applications to data smoothing

## Practice Problems

1. Evaluate: lim(x→2) (x² - 4)/(x - 2)
2. Find: lim(x→∞) (3x² + 2x)/(x² - 1)
3. Determine where f(x) = (x² - 9)/(x - 3) is continuous
