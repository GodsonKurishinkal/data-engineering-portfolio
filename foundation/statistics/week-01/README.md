# Week 1: Introduction to Statistics and Data Types

## Learning Objectives
- Understand the role of statistics in data engineering
- Identify different types of data
- Learn basic statistical terminology
- Introduction to data collection methods

## Topics Covered

### 1. What is Statistics?
- Descriptive vs. Inferential statistics
- Population vs. Sample
- Parameters vs. Statistics
- Applications in data engineering

### 2. Types of Data
- Quantitative vs. Qualitative
- Discrete vs. Continuous
- Nominal, Ordinal, Interval, Ratio scales
- Structured vs. Unstructured data

### 3. Data Collection
- Sampling methods
- Experimental design basics
- Observational studies
- Bias in data collection

## Practice Problems

1. Classify the following as quantitative or qualitative: age, color, temperature, satisfaction rating
2. Identify the scale of measurement: zip code, temperature in Celsius, ranking (1st, 2nd, 3rd)
3. Design a simple random sample plan for surveying 100 students from a university of 10,000
